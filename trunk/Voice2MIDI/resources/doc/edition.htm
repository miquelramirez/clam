Editing

The voice to MIDI conversion system offers the user the possibility of adjusting
the beginning and end times of the notes which form the melody obtained through
the segmentation process. The system also enables the user to delete notes from
the melody where necessary.

These functionalities are accessed through the segmentation and Piano Roll views.
The views are synchronised so any editing actions taken in one of the views will
be reflected in the other.
